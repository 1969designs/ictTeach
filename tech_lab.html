Tech Lab

Welcome to my new site

My name is Keith George: qualified teacher of History who has spent more time teaching ICT.

The main purposes of this website are:

  • as a means to brush up on my web design and HTML & CSS coding skills.
  • as a platform to practice, store & display new& more advanced coding & programing work.
  • as an active library of lessons & resources to use with my students.

Currently this site is still under construction & going through the main initial design phase, which evolves as I'm introduced to new web technologies and practices... & while I make the tricky choice of site name.

This site is being designed to be Responsive to the different ways in which people view the Internet & Accessible, to ensure that as many people as possible can make use of it. Having initially started this site from a desktop perspective I ultimately decided to start again, from scratch, with the "Mobile First" mantra playing on loop. So far, all appears hunky-dory.

On this page I will be providing updates on the work in progress on the site, checking off completed tasks & adding new ones as they emerge. I wil also be linking to examples of the other projects I intend to be working on this year which will include looking into Python & PHP to extend my knowledge & skills of Web Design & Coding.
